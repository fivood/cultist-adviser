-- Endings --
#a_ending_arrest
Get arrested by a hunter (ending: Bars Across the Sun).
During a Season of Suspicion, a hunter may appear while Notoriety or Mystique is on the table; each later Season of Suspicion absorbs Mystique/Notoriety and, depending on the hunter, produces Evidence. Sending followers on cult business risks Notoriety; killing prisoners or letting corpses fade always yields it. Stockpile Notoriety and let a Season of Suspicion absorb it all plus Damning Evidence.
#a_ending_deathofthebody
Die of exhausted Health (ending: Where Will They Find Me?).
Idle without working or healing: let every Health turn into Affliction, Hunger and Injury, then into Decrepitude, and wait out the next Season of Sickness.
#a_ending_despairending
Let Dread pile up to three (ending: No Worst, There Is None).
Failed expeditions and discussing Winter lore with a hunter always yield Dread; working with Decrepitude sometimes does; Restlessness and any Moth/Winter influence decays into Dread. Stockpile three Dread and let the next Season of Despair devour all three.
Note: Contentment cancels Dread inside the Season of Despair — for each Contentment absorbed, prepare one extra Dread.
#a_ending_visionsending
Let Fascination pile up to three (ending: Under An Appreciable Mental Strain).
Discussing Lantern lore or Fascination with a hunter, painting at Work with 6+ inspiration, and any expiring level-6+ Lantern influence all yield Fascination; the Mansus (Lodge of the Sage Knight, Painted River, Chamber of Ways, Red Church, Peacock's Door) can too. Stockpile three and let the next Season of Visions devour them.
Note: Dread cancels Fascination inside the Season of Visions — for each Dread absorbed, prepare one extra Fascination.

Accept Poppy Lascelles' terms but fail to sacrifice a follower (ending: The Clock Striketh Twelve).
Keep slotting lore into Talk until Poppy Lascelles appears. Talk to her with Passion; an action warns she will return with the turning season, and 300 seconds later she demands a follower. Offer no one.

#a_ending_minorforgevictory
Ascend through Forge (ending: Hairless and Imperishable).
Complete any Rite at Work with 36+ total Forge and the Ascension: Power card carrying The Marks of Shaping at 6.
The desire card:
If your starting card is Temptation: Sensation or Temptation: Enlightenment, dream it with Health to turn it into Temptation: Power.
Level 1→2: dream the temptation with level-4+ Forge lore — it becomes Dedication: Power.
Level 2→3: Work the dedication with Way: Through the Stag Door and level-6+ Forge lore — it becomes Ascension: Power.
Level 3→6: each Season of Ambitions draws in the ascension card and tries to devour one Funds; success raises the desire a level, failure lowers it (never below 3).
The rite:
Feed it lore (up to 14), a follower (usually up to 10; great summons reach 12), a tool (up to 12), an influence (up to 15) and an ingredient (up to 12). Examples:
Sunset Rite + a level-10 Forge spell + King Crucible (12) + a level-15 Forge influence;
Rite of the Sea's Feasting + level-12 Forge lore + a level-12 tool + a level-12 ingredient;
Rite of the Mother's Mercy + level-12 Forge lore + King Crucible + a level-12 ingredient.
If a lover gets drawn into the rite midway, do NOT add Passion or you'll get the Ever After ending instead.

-- Summoning --
#a_summon_generic
Successfully summon a spirit.
Run a Rite-aspected card at Work with the required materials and aspect totals. Risen last 120 seconds (extendable by rite); other summons usually last 180 seconds on the table. When their time runs out or they're wounded they return to their world, leaving residue where applicable.
