密教军师 · Cultist Adviser  v0.14.0

==============================
中文更新说明
==============================

本版重点修正终局与召唤的错误判断，并把复杂成就提示提前到真正的路线分叉。
已安装旧版的用户可点击军师顶部更新提示自动升级。

1. 真实终局仪式
- 普通灯、铸、杯飞升需要 36，不再把桌面所有卡牌属性简单相加。
- 逐张验证现有仪式的真实槽位，并列出可以直接照放的完整摆卡与消耗物。
- 杯路线只有献祭恋人时才有 27 的替代配方；行尸不会把门槛降到 27。
- 50 属性配方不能在同一局直接执行，它们指向下一局对应的使徒大胜路线。

2. 真实召唤与资源调度
- 11 种召唤物按仪式槽位逐一验证；没有尸体不会再提示可召唤行尸。
- 就绪项显示完整摆卡、被消耗卡牌、持续时间和失控处理。
- 军师会计算卡牌与行动格何时释放；争用同一资源的建议会标成备选。
- 限时机会若因行动格占用已经赶不上，会直接说明，避免误导为普通排队任务。

3. 三条使徒大胜路线
- 铸：追踪破晓装置核心、蓝金、罗盘、厄客德娜和最终配方。
- 杯：追踪美味之血、七重恩典、宾客名单、操线仙娥和最终宴席。
- 灯：追踪十字路口、诱饵、七名见证者、持钥人和最终仪式。

4. 复杂成就提前规划
- 舞者：从盖提剧院阶段追踪恩主的迷恋/厌倦和求婚倒计时；显示旧形/新形平衡。
- 教士：第一道伤疤前规划健康，明确 3 伤疤牝马与 7 伤疤母亲分叉。
- 食尸鬼：提前说明墓地之口两条路线；淡白画作逐色显示 N/9 和下一份材料。
- 流亡者：起誓前提示隐居与抗命互斥；击杀需 7 伤，抗命飞升只需 6 道永久伤势。
- 只追踪尚未解锁的成就；守密人档仍保持不干预。

验证：143 项自动测试全部通过。

==============================
English Release Notes
==============================

This release corrects endgame and summoning feasibility, and moves difficult
achievement guidance back to the route decisions where it is actually useful.
Existing installations can update from the banner at the top of the adviser.

1. Real rite validation for ascension
- Standard Lantern, Forge and Grail ascensions require 36.
- Every plan is checked against the owned rite's actual slots and lists the
  complete playable layout plus consumed cards.
- Only Grail with a romantic sacrifice has the 27-point alternative. A Risen
  does not lower the threshold.
- The 50-point recipes are not craftable in the same run; they lead to the
  matching Apostle legacy in the next run.

2. Exact summoning and resource scheduling
- All 11 summons are checked against real rite slots. Risen recipes require a corpse.
- Ready summons show the complete layout, consumption, duration and control response.
- Card/verb release times and cross-suggestion resource conflicts are tracked.
- If a busy verb cannot return before a timed opportunity expires, the adviser says so.

3. Three Apostle major-victory routes
- Forge tracks the Dawnbreaker Core, Blue Gold, Compass, Echidna and finale.
- Grail tracks Savorous Blood, Seven Graces, guests, Marinette and the final feast.
- Lantern tracks the Crossroads, Allure, seven Witnesses, Key-holder and finale.

4. Earlier planning for difficult achievements
- Dancer tracks the benefactor from the Gaiety job, proposal deadlines, and
  Old Form/New Form balance.
- Priest plans Health before the first scar and marks the 3-scar/7-scar fork.
- Ghoul explains the Graveyard Mouth fork early and tracks Palest Painting N/9.
- Exile warns that vows close retirement; killing needs 7 wounds, while a
  Defiance ascension needs only 6 permanent foe wounds.
- Completed routes retire automatically; Keeper mode remains hands-off.

Validation: all 143 automated tests pass.
